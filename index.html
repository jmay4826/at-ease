Why therapeutic massage?

Release

Trigger point therapy can release constricted areas in the muscles to reduce pain and improve circulation in the body.

Relief

Deep tissue massage can relieve muscular pain throughout the body, including the upper back, lower back, shoulders and neck.

Relaxation

The calming atmosphere and soothing touch of a therapeutic massage can provide much-needed stress relief and relaxation.

Stephen List

I am truly excited to be a team member at At Ease Therapeutic Massage, Inc. This opportunity allows me to return home to Warner Robins and showcase my massage therapy abilities alongside the other excellent therapists. It is my goal to see that the needs of every client are met with quality care and professionalism at all times."

Stephen lives in Warner Robins, Georgia where he has done so since 1998. He retired from the U.S. Air Force in 2005 as a Master Sergeant specializing in communications. He spent the next seven years as a defense contractor continuing to serve our nation's military in the fields of video teleconferencing and logistics. In the spring of 2012 while still a defense contractor, he began the journey to become a licensed massage therapist pursuing a passion long overdue. Then, unexpectedly in early 2013, he left defense contracting all together and decided to focus solely on his massage therapy studies. By October of 2013, he was licensed and practicing in the state of Georgia.

In January of 2014, he accepted a position with Global Body Works where he practiced both chair and table massage therapy at the Children's hospitals in Atlanta working on the doctors, nurses, and parents of the child patients. In June of 2014, he transitioned to lead therapist at Eagles Landing Wellness and Chiropractic Center working in concert with the excellent chiropractic and wellness staff there. Then, in June of 2016, he accepted a position at 'At Ease' and remains there to this day. He is very skilled at deep tissue, neuromuscular therapy (NMT) and seated massage. He considers those to be his strongest modalities despite holding certifications in others as well.

Stephen was born in Columbus, Ohio, raised in Southbridge, Massachusetts and attended Ohio State University for several years before entering the military. During his 22 year military career that began in 1984, he lived in the United Kingdom, Japan, Germany and several other U.S. locations. He also served multiple tours in the Middle East during the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan. He is married to his wife Carole who hails from the United Kingdom and together they have two grown children, Nicole and Bryan. They also have three grandsons, Christian, Mason, and Chase. He enjoys playing golf, water color, and spending time with his family, who by-the-way, are all die-hard New England Patriots fans since the early 1970's. Go Patriots!
